Pizza

Back to All Recipes | Description Ingredients Steps

Description

An Italian dish consisting in an usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients such as mushrooms, ham, olives, vegetables, etc. It is baked quickly at a very high temperature and served hot.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Dry yeast
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Warm water
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite pizza toppings, e.g., ham or mushrooms

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, yeast, salt, olive oil, and warm water. Knead the dough until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  2. Roll out the dough. On a floured surface, roll out the dough into a thin circle or rectangle.
  3. Add toppings. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough. Add shredded mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings, such as ham or mushrooms.
  4. Bake and serve. Preheat the oven to 230°C. Bake the pizza for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and crispy. Slice and serve hot.
  5. Enjoy!
