Tiramisù

Back to All Recipes | Description Ingredients Steps

Description

A classic coffee-flavoured Italian dessert, made of ladyfingers (savoiardi) dipped in coffee and layered with a whipped cream made of mascarpone cheese, and flavoured with cocoa.

Ingredients

  • Coffee
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Ladyfingers
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Prepare the coffee. Brew a cup of coffee and let it cool down.
  2. Make the cream. In a mixing bowl, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until creamy and pale yellow. Then, add mascarpone cheese and whisk until smooth.
  3. Assemble the layers. Dip ladyfingers into the cooled coffee and arrange them in a single layer in a dish. Then, spread half of the cream mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and cream.
  4. Dust with cocoa. Sprinkle the cocoa powder over the top of the cream layer.
  5. Chill and serve. Refrigerate the dish for at least 4 hours. After that, serve chilled.
  6. Enjoy!
