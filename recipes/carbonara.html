Pasta Carbonara

Back to All Recipes | Description Ingredients Steps

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish made with spaghetti, bacon, eggs, pecorino cheese, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper that creates a creamy and flavorful sauce.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Pecorino cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook bacon. In a skillet, cook the bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove the bacon from the skillet and set aside.
  2. Boil pasta. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until it is al dente.
  3. Prepare sauce. Meanwhile, in a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan and Pecorino cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Combine ingredients. Drain the cooked pasta to the bowl with the sauce, stir, add the bacon and toss until well coated.
  5. Serve into bowls. Divide the pasta carbonara into bowls and garnish with additional grated pecorino cheese.
  6. Enjoy!
